About Us

Reliant Homecare Services provides reliable home watch services for homeowners. We perform regular property visits, look for visible issues, and communicate updates so you can feel confident while away from your home.

Our Services

Routine Home Checks

Regular home visits to check doors, windows, appliances, and overall property condition.

Storm Checks

Additional inspections after severe weather to check for visible damage.

Arrival Preparation

We prepare your home before your return so everything is ready for you.

Concierge Services

We also provide extra services, such as transportation to and from the airport, grocery shopping and restocking your pantry and fridge, before your arrival.
